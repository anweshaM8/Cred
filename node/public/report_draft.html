About The Company
Ideal Sheet Metal Stampings & Pressings Private Limited (ISMSPPL), as per its filings, is engaged in the business of manufacturing of textile spare parts. The company offers simplex bobbins, ring tubes, solid cheese and cone, dye cheese and cones, bobbin holders, and lappet hooks. The company’s clientele includes Vardhman, Damodar Group, GHL, RSWM Limited, and Aditya Birla. The company was incorporated in the year 1962 and its registered office located in Ahmedabad, Gujarat.
